- An accepted `content-plan-YYYY-MM-DD.md` item with `format: brand_post` or `series`
- `raw/ops/posts/_craft.md` (your project's craft/voice process, if you have one)
- Your brand voice/visual canon files (wherever your instance defines them — this stage
  never invents tone or palette, it only reads what you've already defined)

- Write `raw/ops/posts/YYYY-MM-DD-<slug>.md` using your existing posts template/process;
  `Status` field starts at `rascunho`/`draft`.
- Apply your craft process's density/quality checklist per paragraph before presenting the draft.
- Show the draft in chat for approval before marking it reviewed.

## Caps

- Reuses the existing `raw/ops/posts/` template and process as-is — do not invent a
  parallel format.

## Bans

- **No** vendor LLM HTTP from repo tools.
- **Never** publish automatically — founder publishes from their own account.
- **Never** invent tone/palette outside the brand canon.
